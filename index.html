Structure & Knowledge Injection Toward Machine Learning

About us


    The goal of our research is injecting knowledge/constraints into neural models, primarily for natural language processing (NLP) tasks. We are broadly interested in structured prediction, multi-task learning, logical reasoning, and better representation learning for the aforementioned topics.

    While Machine Learning is a great framework to learn a black-box model that best represents given training set, sometimes the model fails to learn simple rules that is necessary for safety or logical reasoning. However, if we step back and think, not all the aspects of the model need to be learned from the data itself as some constraints of the model are readily available to us.
